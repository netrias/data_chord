Stage 2 — guided mapping prototypes

Two takes on a more guided Stage 2, sharing the same seed data (a 25-column oncology cohort with realistic AI-rec ambiguity).

1
Concierge — maximum lock-in
App triages everything. User walks through only the columns that need attention. No queue, no jumping, one decision at a time. Strong rewrite warnings. End-of-flow summary lets users audit auto-confirmed columns.
Open → 2
Walkthrough — guided with flexibility
Same per-column card, plus a queue sidebar. Users can jump to any row, flag for follow-up, and toggle auto-safe columns into the queue if they want. More escape hatches without losing the funnel.
Open → 3
Value-first — two-card, sorted by impact
Two large cards (your column ↔ target standard) with a giant value-match hero. Columns sorted into four sections: will rewrite (worst overlap first), already matches, pass-through, empty. Sign off after section one if you're satisfied — no need to click through 86 columns.
